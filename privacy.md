Privacy Policy for Artemis II Screensaver

Effective Date: April 16, 2026

Artemis II Screensaver (“the App”) is a Roku screensaver channel that displays a curated set of images. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains what data is (and is not) collected.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

No account information is required
No personal data is collected
No analytics or tracking tools are used
2. Internet Connectivity

The App does not require an internet connection to function.

All images and assets are included within the installed application
The App does not make any external network requests
3. Data Storage

The App does not store any data on your device beyond what is required for normal Roku operation.

No user preferences are saved
No viewing history is tracked
No local or remote databases are used
4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, including:

Analytics providers
Advertising networks
External APIs
5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children or users of any age.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, updates will be reflected here with a new effective date.
