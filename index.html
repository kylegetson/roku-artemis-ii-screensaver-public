Unofficial Artemis II Screensaver

Bring Artemis II to Your Roku Screen

A fan-made Roku screensaver built around NASA imagery from one of the most exciting missions on the path back to the Moon.

Free to install. To add it to your Roku, visit the Roku Channel Store and click Add channel.

See Artemis II Screensaver on the Roku Store

Artemis II Screensaver is an unofficial fan-made project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by NASA or the Artemis program.

About the Screensaver

Unofficial Artemis II Screensaver turns an idle Roku display into a simple gallery of mission imagery, giving the TV a little bit of launch-day energy instead of a blank screen. It is designed to be lightweight, easy to install, and focused on the visuals.

The idea is straightforward: celebrate the Artemis II mission with a screensaver that feels clean, calm, and inspiring while the room is quiet. If you like spaceflight, crewed exploration, or just beautiful NASA imagery, it is a fun little tribute.

Image Source

The images featured in the screensaver are sourced from NASA's Artemis II multimedia page, which collects official mission imagery and related media.
